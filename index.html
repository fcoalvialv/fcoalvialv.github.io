Francisco J. Alvial Vásquez

Is a geophysicist and doctoral student in physics. In particular, he focuses on the prediction, attribution and impact of hydro-climatic phenomena, through the use of observations, satellite data, reanalysis products, simulations and their evaluation in hydrological models.

I currently belong to the doctoral program in physical sciences of the Physics Department of the Universidad de Concepción, Concepción, Chile.
